Rufino App - Android Application for Parque Rufino Tamayo

School Project Social Service

Rufino App was intended to solve a problem that "El Patronato del Parque Rufino Tamayo" was having. They couldn't follow all the problems that were happening in their installations. Rufino App then was designed to even a reporting system. This app lets users report incidences of the park with different categories. The park visitants could check their report status and know when the problem is solved. The administrators were able to check all the incidences in the park and update their status.
Develop using Kotlin, NodeJS, Express, and MongoDB. Responsible for the App frontend and backend development in Android Studio.

Together with Carlos Lucio, Carlos Mora, Elmer Ávila, and Josue Cano.

Together with Andrés Piñones, Edith Benvenuto, Fabrizzio Ramírez, and José Cruz.

Cemex Valley - Web Application for CEMEX

School Project

This web application was intended to improve the performance of software developers at CEMEX based on KPIs by gamification through the pages and a videogame. Cemex Valley (the videogame) is integrated with the work of the software developers.
The gameplay is related to the tasks software developers need to do. Whenever a task was approved, they built a building. When there were many buildings, they ended up with a city. They also see how many cities have been finished by wandering in the world.
Develop with Bootstrap 4, JavaScript, NodeJS, and SQL Server. Responsible for the videogame development in Unity and integration with the web.

Platzi SpaceMan - Platformer Game

Personal Project

Platzi SpaceMan is a basic platformer game which objective is to go the furthest possible and collect coin to improve your score. It's part of a course by Platzi with Juan Gabriel Gomila.
Here I applied concepts such as the good practices for importing assets, create prefabs, animations, procedural level design, scene management, and the fundamentals of C# for Unity.
Controls:
Movement Left and Right: "A" & "D" or "←" & "→"
Jump: "Space" or "Left Click"
Super Jump: "C" or "right Click"
To deactivate audio after playing just click outside the game.
